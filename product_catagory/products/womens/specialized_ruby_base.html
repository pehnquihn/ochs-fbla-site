Specialized 2014 Women's Ruby Base Road Bike - $1,724.99
Description
The 2014 Specialized Ruby Base Women’s Road Bike is a quick and efficient, FACT carbon bike, with Zertz vibration dampers in the frame and fork, designed to cover long distances in maximum control and comfort. High-quality Shimano Sora compact drivetrain and DT Axis 1.0 wheelset provide value and performance. Designed with women’s specific geometry in mind, this bike is more like a diamond than a ruby; a girls best friend.
