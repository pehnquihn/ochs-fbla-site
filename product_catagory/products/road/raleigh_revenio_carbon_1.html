Raleigh 2014 Revenio Carbon 1 Road Race Bike - $1,699.99
Description
This is your perfect upgrade from that heavier aluminum bike you’ve been dragging along the pavement the last few years. Bridging the gap to the world of carbon-framed road machines, the 2014 Raleigh Revenio Carbon 1 welcomes you into this new frontier with a smooth, comfortable, light weight transition. With features like Shimano’s Tiagra 10-speed set-up, compact chainrings for easy pedaling, and our Momentum double wall rims for extra durability. This entry-level bike in our Revenio Carbon line offers up a choice package for taking your game to the next level. This bike might make you a faster rider and it might get you a few more high fives from your riding friends, but it will certainly put a smile on your face and help keep enough dough in your bank account for that post-ride refreshment.
