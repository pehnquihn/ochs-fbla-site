Dahon 2014 Mariner D7 Folding Bike - $549.99
Description
A frequent winner of folding bike shootouts that include bikes that cost up to four times as much, the 2014 Dahon Mariner D7 Folding Bike is one of Dahon’s most popular bicycles. It’s a high quality, lightweight folding bike at a surprisingly affordable price. Originally designed specifically for boaters, the Mariner D7’s portability makes this bike ideal urban bike commutes that include a stretch by bus or train.
