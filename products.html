What Bike is Right for You?

Each type of bike has its own advantages and disadvantages. If you are new to the hobby, then you should take time to think of what you would like to do with your bike. Figure out if you will be using your bike for racing or leisure. You should also thing of what type of path you will be riding on. Different bikes are built for different terrains.

Road Bikes

Road bikes have a focus on efficiency and speed. Most Road bikes are very comfortable and a pleasure to ride. Due to how fast the bikes are on streets they are the choice for those who wish to use their bike to race.

Go to Road Bikes
Mountain Bikes

Mountain Bikes are the kings of off-road riding. They are built to be able to take on any type of path you ride it one. If you are looking to take your bike to extreme places this is where you should start.

Go to Mountain Bikes
Cyclocross Bikes

Cyclocross is a very demanding sport there for it demands a top notch bike. These bikes will be very good with paved roads, off road, and any incline or decline you can think of. Cycolcross bikes are the perfect mix of rugged build along with a light weight package. If you want to participate in this high endurance sport these bikes are a must have.

Go to Cyclocross Bikes
Folding Bikes

Folding bikes are a great alternative to the traditional bike for those who commute by bike and public transport. They fold up into a light weight small package that can be carried with you if you need to get on a bus or train on your way. Due to the size of the folding bike when it is folded and their high quality design, they are also very nice for travelers.

Go to Folding Bikes
Electric-assist Bikes

Electric-assist bikes are very versatile. They are becoming very popular with people in urban areas. Many people are starting to use them to replace their cars when they are commuting to work or running errands. Electric-assist bikes allow people to travel at faster speeds with little work. When you need to park, bikes take up less space than cars. Also with todayâ€™s high gas prices being able to just plug these bikes in and have them ready to go again is much cheaper and more convenient.

Go to Electric-assist Bikes
Women's Bikes

Woman's bikes are tailored to the smaller bodies of women. Besides the smaller size most other things are the same. These bikes are perfect for anyone who just feels other bike types are too large.

Go to Woman's Bikes
Children Bikes

These bikes are a great way to get your kids active and outdoors. For the most part these bikes are also much more affordable than other bike types.

Go to Children Bikes
